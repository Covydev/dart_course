Course 8: Collections

In almost every application you make, you’ll be dealing with
collections of data. Data can be organized in multiple ways,
each with a different purpose. Dart provides multiple
solutions to fit your collection’s needs, and in this topic
you’ll learn about three of the main ones: lists, sets and
maps.

1. Lists
2. Sets 
3. Maps 
4. Higher order methods
5. When to use lists, sets or maps
